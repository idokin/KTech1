If you think you need help please:


Unless you know what you are doing, improper techniques often make recovery more difficult and sometimes result in permanent data loss.

Stay calm, do not panic and call a professional data recovery company.

DO NOT try to solve the problem yourself.
DO NOT format your drive.
DO NOT run scandisk.
DO NOT run utility programs.
 

Better safe than sorry - here are some helpful tips:


• Always backup important information and data.
• Use good anti-virus application to detect any virus in your  drive. Sometimes by killing
   the virus and reboot, the problems may go away.
• Do not allows your drive to experience extreme hot or cold  temperature.
• Avoid resting your computer on unstable or movable objects.
• Avoid leaving your drive unattended at places prone to water,  fire or magnetic field.

The advice listed above is from our own personal experience. By no means should any advice on this site be a guarantee to solve the problem. You should always have an expert work on your computer. We will not be responsible for damage caused by anyone acting upon the advice above.
