Password tips

“Treat your password like your toothbrush. Don’t let anybody use it, and get a new one every 6 months”


  • A strong password is your first line of defense against hackers.
  • Never give out your password to anyone
  • Use a unique password for each of your important accounts.
  • Make the password at least 8 characters long.
  • Include numbers, capital letters and symbols.
